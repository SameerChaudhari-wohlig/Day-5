ABOUT US

Petrauskas Trading Ltd, your local family run business.

Petrauskas Trading Ltd is a family run construction company who are dedicated to providing the best professional building services including a range of domestic and commercial renovations from bathroom refurbishment, kitchen modernization, building extensions, basements and loft conversions, period refurbishments, dwelling separation, gardens makeovers.

Evolving within the construction industry Petrauskas Trading have acquired a wealth of knowledge which we have honed to provide a service that separates us from our competitors and maximizes the benefits to our clients. Through our experience working within high end construction and continuously evolving expertise we now bring to the private domestic consumer, high end cutting edge on trend design ideas and build solutions using the latest and eco-friendly solutions at an affordable price.

How we operate: From our first meeting with you we take the time to understand your vision and then with our hassle free conception to completion service we will offer you not only the solutions to achieve your desired outcome but in the most cost effective way whilst never compromising on quality in workmanship.

Petrauskas Trading team management along with our accredited site managers, carefully hand pick members from our workforce to put together the perfect combination of different trades men to specifically carry out your project whilst managing and overseeing the project from start to finish to ensure our standards are consistent and always advancing to deliver every time.

We offer a complete service for not only the homeowner but for the professionals within construction, having worked with many architects and surveyors over the years we understand the importance of a professional hassle free service which is vital for client relations. Experience covers detailed tender returns and project management working on project values of up to 1M we are able to cover all aspects of the project using where necessary bona fide fully insured subcontractors to see the project through from start to finish.

Word of mouth and repeat business has made Petrauskas Trading what it is today and we hope you will allow us the opportunity to show you how our complete service can help your project to be hassle free and swiftly come to fruition.

Through our good relationships built with our clients we can also facilitate access to relative past projects for you to view as required.
